Running a Supermarket Is Painful

Most Nigerian supermarket owners are bleeding money every month — not because their store is failing, but because they're managing it the hard way. Here's what's costing you:

📞

Orders by Phone Call

Your phone rings all day. Customers call to ask prices, check stock, place orders. Your staff spends more time answering calls than packing. Orders get mixed up. Customers get frustrated.

Costs you: TIME + CUSTOMERS
🗑️

Products Expire Unsold

Nobody is tracking expiry dates closely enough. By the time someone notices, the yoghurt, bread, or milk has already expired. You throw it away. That's pure cash in the bin.

Costs you: ₦40,000/month
💸

Missing Cash From Deliveries

Driver delivers an order for ₦8,500. He comes back with ₦6,000 and a story. You have no record of what was collected. No way to verify. The money is just... gone.

Costs you: ₦25,000/month
📋

Manual Stock Counting

Every week, staff spend 10+ hours physically counting inventory. They write numbers in a book. The book gets lost or has errors. You never really know what you have.

Costs you: 10+ hours/week
🌑

No Visibility After Hours

Once you leave the store, you're blind. You can't take orders, you can't check stock levels, you can't see what's happening. Your store stops existing when you're not there.

Costs you: LOST SALES
🤷

No Idea What's Selling

You restock based on guesswork. You buy too much of products that sit on shelves and not enough of the fast movers. You're making purchasing decisions blind.

Costs you: POOR DECISIONS

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Straight answers to the questions we get asked most.

Do my customers need to download an app?

▼
No app download needed. The customer store is a website that works on any phone browser — iPhone or Android. You share the link on WhatsApp, Instagram, or paste it in your bio, and customers can start ordering immediately.

What happens to my existing stock — do I have to enter everything again?

▼
Yes, products need to be added to the system — but we handle this during setup. On the Professional and Enterprise plans, we set everything up for you. On the Starter plan, we give you a guide and tools to import your inventory quickly. Most stores are fully loaded within a day.

Can I use this if I don't do delivery — only pickup?

▼
Absolutely. The system supports pickup-only mode. Customers can place orders online, come to the store to collect, and pay at the counter. The driver portal simply won't be needed if you don't do delivery — but it's there if you ever want to start.

What if my internet goes down?

▼
The system requires internet to work — just like any website. We recommend having a backup data connection (a second SIM or mobile hotspot) for the staff tablet or computer. Most store operations can continue manually for short outages, and everything syncs when connection is restored.

Is this a monthly subscription or a one-time payment?

▼
One-time payment. You own the system outright. There are no monthly fees. The only recurring cost is web hosting, which is either free (on our servers) or very low cost if you want your own custom domain (around ₦5,000/year).

How long does setup take?

▼
On the Professional plan (where we do everything), your store is typically live within 2–3 days. This includes setting up all 4 portals, uploading your products, configuring your passwords, and training your staff. On the Starter plan, setup time depends on you — typically 1–2 weeks if you follow our guide.

Can I try it before paying?

▼
Yes. The live demo at grandpas-dream-supermarket.vercel.app is a real, fully functional version of the customer store. You can browse it, add items, and even place a test order right now. For the staff and manager portals, book a free 15-minute demo call and we'll walk you through everything live.
